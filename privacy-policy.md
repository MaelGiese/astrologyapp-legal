# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how AstrologyApp collects, uses, and shares your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Profile Information:** Name, email address (optional)
- **Birth Information:** Birth date, birth time, and birth location (required for astrological calculations)
- **Partner Information:** Birth details for relationship compatibility analysis
- **Chat Data:** Messages and questions you send through the chat feature
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features accessed, time spent in app

**Third-Party Services:** RevenueCat (subscriptions), Chat Services (horoscope generation), Expo Platform (notifications)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Generate birth charts and horoscopes
- Provide personalized astrological content
- Process chat requests and questions
- Maintain and improve app features
- Send notifications (only if you opt in)
- Manage your subscription

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** Most data is stored locally on your device
- **Encryption:** Data transmitted between your device and servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Security:** We implement measures to protect your information against unauthorized access

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

- **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who help operate the app (chat services, RevenueCat)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly consent

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update your profile information in app settings
- **Deletion:** Delete all local data using "Clear All Local Data" in settings
- **Opt-Out:** Disable notifications or features at any time

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Local Data:** Stored on your device until you clear it or uninstall
- **Server Logs:** Automatically deleted after 30 days
- **Chat History:** Stored locally only; cleared when you delete the app

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

**Email:** privacy@astrologyapp.com

We will respond within 30 days.
